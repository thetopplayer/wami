Why Wami

In today's digital world, the ability to manage our identity is extremely important. Individuals, small businesses, large businesses, and institutions, all have multiple identity profiles that need to be managed securely. WAMI gives you the ability to manage multiple identity profiles each tailored to a specific need or goal letting others know exactly what you want them to know about you, then help you find, connect, and manage these identities and resources.

What is Wami

A WAMI, ("Who am I"), is a virtual profile, and can be created for any person, place or thing, supporting most media types, (audio, video, pics, text, etc). WAMI's are collected in a WAMI folder residing on any smart device and desktop computer. WAMI's can be shared or transmitted to other WAMI folders or to email addresses. WAMI's can be organized into groups for easy access. The WAMI Identity Profile Network can then be searched to find the resource you or your business might be looking for.
